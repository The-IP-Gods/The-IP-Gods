This page contains statistics about the historical trends related to patent approval time.

From this data, it can be seen that:

  • Innovation patents are processed much faster than standard patents
  • The average standard patent processing time is 4.09 years
  • The category of patent with the fastest processing time is ELEC 3 - Computing, with an average time of 3.50 years.
  • The category of patent with the longest processing time is CHEM 1 - Biotechnology
  • Patents in the first half of the year are slightly faster than patents in the second half of the year
